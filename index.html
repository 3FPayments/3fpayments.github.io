How It Works

  1. Self-custody your wealth in your own wallet
  2. Transfer the crypto that you want to spend to your 3F Financial Spending Wallet*
  3. Make a purchase using your Crypto Freedom Debit Card
  4. Your crypto is sold on our Special Purpose Exchange at the current market price
  5. Money is transmitted to the merchant to pay for your purchase
  6. We specifically identify the crypto that you sold according to your tax allocation preference
